# Badge

A compact status or label chip. It comes in three variants across every tone,
two sizes, and an optional leading status dot.

<Demo name="badge/tones" center />

## Variants

`variant` sets the visual emphasis: `solid`, `soft`, or `outline`.

<Demo name="badge/variants" />

## Tones

`tone` carries semantic meaning. Built-in tones are `primary`, `neutral`,
`success`, `warning`, `danger`, and `info` — and any custom tone works too.

<Demo name="badge/tones" />

## Dot

Set `dot` to add a small leading status dot, handy for presence and state.

<Demo name="badge/dot" />
