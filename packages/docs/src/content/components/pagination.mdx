# Pagination

A page navigator backed by the Zag.js pagination machine. Give it the total
`count` and `pageSize` and the machine computes the page list, the prev/next
state, and where to collapse pages behind an ellipsis.

<Demo name="pagination/basic" center />

## Basic

`count` is the total number of items and `pageSize` is how many fit on a page;
together they decide the page count. `siblingCount` controls how many pages sit on
each side of the current one.

<Demo name="pagination/basic" center />

## Many pages

With a large `count` and a higher `siblingCount`, the machine keeps the active
page centered and collapses the rest behind ellipses.
