# Checkbox

A soft, square control with a smoothly drawn check, backed by the Zag.js checkbox
machine. It supports checked, unchecked, and indeterminate states.

<Demo name="checkbox/basic" center />

## States

The machine tracks `checked`, `indeterminate`, and `disabled`. Pass
`defaultChecked` for uncontrolled usage or `checked` with `onCheckedChange` to
control it.

<Demo name="checkbox/basic" center />

## Sizes

`size` scales the box and its check from `sm` to `lg`.
